Artist Statement

We had two zombies (two goggles) because we had more people than the others. Our mission was to create two robots that delivered food by relaying delivery zombies. Our team members had to use all the hardware provided to us to communicate with zombies. For example, the role of informing zombies on the LCD screen of distance with an ultrasonic sensor. Another screen used numerical data to warn zombies. We show danger by moving the bar to the center if there is an obstacle. In addition, to warn that there is an obstacle in front of them in a different sense, an IR sensor was indicated with the alarm system specified for each of the left and right hands.
