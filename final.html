Final Robot Assignment


Dec 16, 2022

Artist Statement

There are many cases where modern people cannot drink water on time in life. I got an idea from this and made my robot. My robot delivers water to modern people when they need water Of course it's not perfect, but my robot does it to some extent. This final project really gave me a lot of trouble. I ordered Maker PI 2040, unlike my classmates. It was my first time using it, and for this reason, I had a lot of coding problems that could operate the robot and connect sensors to the board. I had a lot of difficulties while doing this assignment, but I also learned a lot from it. Although the robot in my project was not perfect, I am really proud of myself for not giving up until the end and struggling to make my robot.
